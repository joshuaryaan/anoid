Seatbelt Dress

Designed and made for a performance by Jaxon Augustus

Smoking Section

Performer: Jaxon Augustus

Model: Jessy Kenny

Music: Smoking Section - St Vincent

Jaxon is a friend and performer, they were talking to me about an upcoming performance at another close friends party and we collaborated on this costume. They wanted a garment that spoke well to the emotional depth of the song and narrative behind the performance. This is constrained, sad, defeated.
